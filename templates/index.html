About PhishShield

PhishShield is an intelligent phishing detection platform designed to protect users from malicious links, fake websites, and social engineering attacks.

How PhishShield Works

  • URL structure and domain reputation analysis
  • SSL certificate and HTTPS validation
  • Keyword and brand impersonation detection
  • Redirection and hosting behavior analysis
  • Risk scoring using weighted security indicators
